BIOGRAPHY

Bio

Mr.Mbinji is an Artistic Director of chorale activities as well as an adjudicator based in Nairobi,Kenya.
He Studied music at Daystar University and during his teaching career has included both classroom and choral music education.
Currently He is a Music teacher at Karura Adventist School, Director of Music at Parklands SDA Church and Artistic Director of Jobel Chorale (Which used to be Jubilee Chorale) that He is the co-founder.
Jobel Chorale is committed to global-style programming, interdenominational performance projects, and innovative collaborations across the city.
Most of his Arrangements and Compositions have featured as set-pieces in different festivals.
His choirs have won numerous international and national awards.
